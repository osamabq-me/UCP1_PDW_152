My trips

My life in Saudi 🕋

I moved to Saudi Arabia when my father relocated for work there. While studying high school there, my passion for technology flourished. I had access to a wide range of technologies and fast internet, which allowed me to easily explore and develop in this field.

My life in Turkey

I embarked on an incredible journey to Turkey, driven by my burning desire to immerse myself in their language and discover the perfect university. With unwavering determination, I turned my childhood dream to travel to turkey into a remarkable reality, and the sheer exhilaration I feel now is absolutely electrifying!

My life in Indonesia

Receiving the news of my scholarship approval at UMY filled me with immense joy. It opened doors to studying Information Technology and discovering new cultures. I embraced a new language and formed lifelong friendships. Additionally, I connected with nature through camping and mountain hiking in Indonesia, creating unforgettable experiences. It's a beautiful and joyous chapter of my life!
